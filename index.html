üõë Steps to Stay Away from Cyber Fraud & Scams

  1. Never Share Secrets: Never share OTPs, PINs, or CVV with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank.
  2. Challenge Urgency: Ignore messages demanding immediate payment or threatening account blocks.
  3. Inspect URLs: Hover over links to see destinations. Watch for typos like micros0ft.com.
  4. Verify All Offers: Job or loan offers that seem too good to be true are almost always scams.
  5. Confirm Money Requests: If a friend asks for money via chat, call them on a known number to verify.
  6. Beware of Remote Access: Never grant remote access (AnyDesk) unless you initiated the request via verified support.

Recognise Common Scam Types

  • Phishing: Fake emails/login pages designed to steal credentials.
  • Vishing: Voice calls asking for OTPs or remote access (pretending to be bank staff).
  • Ransomware: Software that encrypts your files and demands payment.
  • Social Engineering: Manipulating you into giving up KYC or personal info.
